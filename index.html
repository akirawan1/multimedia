About the Project

Art Meets Technology

LUMINA is a multidisciplinary creative studio merging cinematography, generative art, spatial audio, and interactive design. We believe the most powerful stories are those that engage all the senses simultaneously.

Founded in 2018, our collective of directors, composers, and technologists has produced work for international film festivals, brand campaigns, and immersive installations across five continents.
